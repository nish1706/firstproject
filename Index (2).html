Brownie recipe

    Ingredients

    • 2 cups of sugar
    • 1 and half cup of all purpose flour
    • 1 cup butter, melted
    • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
    • half teaspoon of baking powder
    • half teaspoon of salt
    • half cup of walnut

    Directions

      Step1

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.

      2. Step2

    2. Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined. Spread the batter into the prepared pan. Decorate with walnut

      3. Step3
